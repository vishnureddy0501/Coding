White Space:
-----------
https://tailwindcss.com/docs/white-space


whitespace-normal: spaces, newlines not preserved. Wrap Text.
whitespace-nowrap: spaces, newlines not preserved. No wrap.
whitespace-pre: spaces, newlines preserved. No Wrap.
whitespace-pre-line: new lines preserved. spaces not preserved. wrap text.
whitespace-pre-wrap: spaces, newlines preserved. Wrap Text.
whitespace-break-spaces: spaces, newlines preserved. White spaces at the end of line will be wrapped to next line.
