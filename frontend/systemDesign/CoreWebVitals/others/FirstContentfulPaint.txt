First Contentful Paint:
-----------------------
FCP measures the time from navigation until the browser renders the first piece of content.

That “content” can be:
	Text
	Image
	SVG
	Canvas
	Background image

📌 It does NOT include:
	White background
	Spinner without content
	Empty layout


What Causes Poor FCP?
🧠 Core reason:
	The browser cannot paint until critical resources are ready.
